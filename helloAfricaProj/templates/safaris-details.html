Here is Detailed Itenary (Click to extend)

Day 1: Kigali City Tour  

After breakfast at your hotel in Kigali, you will be picked up by our driver guide who will give you a briefing on what your 6 days Rwanda double gorilla trek and chimpanzee tour is about. Later, drive to southwestern Rwanda to Nyungwe Forest National Park a home to 13 distinct primate species, 310 bird species, numerous plant and tree species among others. Arrive early enough to check in at a lodge in Nyungwe Forest National Park.

Day 2: Gorilla Trekking At Ruhengeri  

Make adequate preparation early enough, have breakfast and after, carry some snacks plus bottled water. Head up to Kinigi to be briefed on Rwanda Gorilla Trekking guidelines by an expert from the park who also will allocate to you a gorilla family from 13 groups in Volcanoes National Park. When it clocks 8:00am, a knowledgeable guide leads you into the bamboo forest to spend about 2-6 hours searching for mountain gorillas. Once they have been located, you will be permitted 1 hour to be with these unique species

Day 3: Golden Monkey Trekking  

After a morning tasty cup of tea/coffee, meet and greet your guide then connect to Kinigi to be briefed on Golden monkey Tracking guidelines. Today, your tour highlight is all about tracking to see golden monkeys in the wild and this will be 2-4 hours search including 1 hour of face to face encounter. Return to check out of the booked lodge and be transferred back to Kigali for departure flight
